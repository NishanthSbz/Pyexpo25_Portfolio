FAQ's

Who can participate?

The hackathon is open to all students from the first year of every department. Anyone interested in learning and innovating with Python is welcome.

01

Can I form my team with students from other departments?

Of course, interdisciplinary teams are allowed. However, the team will be classified under the team leader's department.

02

Can my friends from other college can apply for the event?

Unfortunately, no. This is an intra-college event.

03

What if I don’t know how to code?

No problem! Enthusiasm and the willingness to learn are far more important than your current skill level.

04

What are the prizes to be won?

Exciting prizes will be announced soon! Stay tuned for updates!

05

Can we apply as a team?

Yes! You can form teams of 4-6 members, with a mix of design and development skills.
