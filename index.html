About PYEXPO 25

PYEXPO, an exclusive hackathon event conducted at KGISL Institute of Technology. Organized by the IPS Tech Community, this event provides first-year students with an exciting opportunity to explore the world of hackathons, an experience that many have never encountered before. The IPS Tech Community is committed to empowering students by fostering innovation, collaboration, and technical growth.

Purpose of PyExpo 25
The primary goal of PyExpo 25 is to introduce first-year students to the collaborative, problem-solving nature of hackathons. It offers them a platform to nurture their technical skills, promote innovative thinking, and encourage teamwork. By participating in PyExpo 25, students gain hands-on experience in real-world problem-solving while being guided and mentored by seniors and experienced faculty members.

Roadmap of PYEXPO'25

Explore the roadmap of PyExpo'25, guiding participants through each phase,

where the best projects are showcased and celebrated.

  • IDEA EVALUATION
    Date : Dec 19-20, 2024
    Experience the exciting journey of PyExpo'25 as we guide participants from preparation and idea development to the grand finale, showcasing innovation and talent at every step.
  • IDEA2EXPO
    Date : Feb 17-18 ,2025
    Faculty mentors evaluate the practical feasibility and trends of ideas. The top 100 teams proceed to the next stage.
  • HACKSHOPS2EXPO
    Date : Feb 19-20 ,2025
    A two-day intensive workshop for selected teams to refine their ideas and enhance their skills.
  • HACKATHON2EXPO
    Date : Feb 21-22 ,2025
    A 30-hour hackathon where teams develop and execute their ideas in a competitive environment.
  • PITCH2EXPO
    Date : Feb 28 ,2025
    The remaining 50 teams present their projects to a panel of judges, including participants from KGCAS, KiTE, and IIM.
  • PYEXPO'25 - The Grand Finale
    Date : Mar 1 ,2025
    The top projects are showcased to industry evaluators, who choose the best three teams. Winners are awarded and celebrated.
